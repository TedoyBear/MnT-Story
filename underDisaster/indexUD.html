Under Disaster

A Story of Millions of Under Disaster Game Players On Earth Cruel Fantasy World

Synopsis

In the midst of the hustle and bustle of the Earth in 2204, where technology has been integrated with every aspect of life, live four siblings who dedicate their lives to one goal: gaming. They are legends in cyberspace, conquering every digital challenge with extraordinary expertise. However, destiny has a larger plan than just a pixel and keyboard.

A shocking lottery victory changed everything. At the same time, a new phenomenon shakes the virtual universe: Underdisaster, a revolutionary MMORPG that promises unparalleled immersive experience. Without hesitation, the four siblings decided to invest all their lottery prizes into the game, determined to be the strongest, carving their names in the history of Underdisaster.

They succeeded. With a clever strategy and unmatched cooperation, they transformed into unstoppable strengths, dominating every RAID and defeating every enemy. However, when Underdisaster entered season 5, an unexpected event shook the foundation of reality.

Mysteriously, all Underdisaster players on Earth were declared dead. However, this is not the end, but the beginning of the real adventure. Their eyes were open in an amazing fantasy world, found themselves trapped in the in-game character they had built with difficulty.

Can these four siblings survive in this foreign world? Will the strength they have enough to face a greater threat than just a boss in the game? Divide this epic story, where the boundary between reality and fantasy blurred, and true adventure has just begun!
